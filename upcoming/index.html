Upcoming

Last updated: December 16, 2023.

This page gives a rough overview of some upcoming games, their progress, and expected timelines.

The dates given on this page are only estimates and they may change at any time.

Circadian Loop

Circadian Loop is a short, free, probably kinetic visual novel. It contains themes of tragedy and violence, as well as time travel.

The sprites, backgrounds, writing and GUI are more or less finished. CGs are currently being worked on. Music will come after.

More information about the game itself will be posted closer to the release date.

ETA: Q1-Q2 2024.

Sable's Grimoire 2

Taking place in Sable's second year at Amadronia Academy, this game is a direct sequel to the original Sable's Grimoire. It follows After Story, the final expansion to the first game, and shouldn't be played until you've already completed it.

The sprites are almost done, though more outfits and such will likely be added as time goes on.

Backgrounds are currently being worked on, but still in the early stages.

Work on the CGs and music will start later. Probably Q1 or Q2 2024.

A new GUI is likely in store, but work has not started on this yet.

Writing is ongoing. Significant progress has been made, but this is a very long, branching game, so there's always more to be done here.

The Steam store page is live here:

ETA: 2024-2025, depending on how many routes are in the initial release vs how many are added later on through updates.

Sable's Grimoire: Project Homonculus

*Note: this logo is not final and may change prior to release.

Project Homonculus is a sequel to Sable's Grimoire: A Dragon's Treasure.

Work will not fully begin on this until after the initial release of Sable's Grimoire 2.

ETA: 2025-2026, depending on when SG2 releases.

Sable's Grimoire: My Elven Bride

My Elven Bride is a sequel to Sable's Grimoire: Man And Elf.

Work will not fully begin on this until after the release of Project Homonculus.

ETA: 2026-2027, depending on when Project Homonculus releases.
